Celebrity collaboration

Flo Mattress — building mass appeal through celebrity storytelling

Challenge: mattresses are a high-consideration D2C category — shoppers were unsure about comfort and value without trying the product first.

Approach: paired a celebrity face with macro, lifestyle, and regional creators for relatable storytelling. Content led with routines, comfort, and wellness rather than product specs, and explained the benefits simply before amplifying across social and brand channels.
